Mochabear Industries – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

Overview

Mochabear Industries builds fitness and training applications, including Vector. We do not collect, store, share, sell, or transmit personal data in any of our apps. All data remains on your device’s local storage.

Data Collection

Our applications:

  • do not collect personal information
  • do not collect usage analytics
  • do not track users across apps or websites
  • do not use advertising or third-party tracking SDKs
  • do not collect device identifiers (including IDFA)

Data Storage

Any preferences, history, or settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may open external links (for example, instructional videos hosted on platforms such as YouTube). These services have their own privacy policies. Mochabear Industries does not share user data with them.

Children’s Privacy

Mochabear Industries does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes

If this privacy policy changes, it will be updated here before any data collection behavior changes.
